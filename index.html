STORY

Hi, I'm Aishvi Mehta

Mumbai-based designer with a passion for visual communication and graphics.

Design is all about simplifying complex ideas and transforming them into impactful, creative solutions. I find inspiration in experiences around me, translating them into tangible expressions that resonate with people.

My work is rooted in a strong aesthetic sense, an eye for detail, and a drive for perfection. Whether I'm crafting a brand identity, illustrating a story, or innovating a product, I approach every project with a deep appreciation for how design connects us to our surroundings and ourselves.
